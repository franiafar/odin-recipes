Fernet Cocktail

Description

Classic argentinan drink. Well paired with beef meat and friends.

Ingredients

  • 60ml. Branca Fernet
  • Ice
  • Coca Cola to file

Recipe

  1. Add the 60ml. of Branca Fernet in a cup with ices. Let it chill.
  2. Fill the cup with Coca Cola. Let the spume reduce.
  3. Enjoy!
