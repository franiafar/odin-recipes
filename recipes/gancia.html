Gancia Cocktail

Description

Classic and historic ¿cuban? cocktail, known for it's kicky rum and mint accent.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 8 mint leafs
  • 30ml. lime juice
  • 60ml. dark rum

Recipe

  1. Mix the sugar, mint leaves and lime juice in the cup
  2. Break them up with a mortar
  3. Add rum and ice as wanted
